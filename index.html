Our Formats

Sedna FM

Sedna FM episodes are our long-form episodes, where Yasmin and Gaia explore topics in depth, letting the music evolve and shape the narrative. Featuring 10 tracks for a total of one hour per episode, published on a monthly basis. Ideal for a long run, grocery shopping, or cleaning your apartment. You can find it by clicking channel 2 on the radio above, or by clicking on the image or title of this format.

Morning Drops

Morning Drops is our special rubric for Monday mornings, designed to help you start the day and the week with the right energy while discovering new music and new facts. Published every Monday morning at 5am CET, with a duration of about 15 to 20 minutes each. You can find it here by clicking channel 1 (CH1) on the radio, or by clicking on the image or title of this format.

Evening Flows

Evening Flows is a space for selectors, DJs, and artists to share music sets that unearth hidden gems from their personal libraries—sounds that reflect their own world. No rules, no limits. Just music that tells a story. Find it on channel 3 (CH3) of the radio image above, or by clicking on the title or image of this format.

On The Go

Sedna On The Go is another way to expand. We grab our microphones, show up at music stores or random places, and talk to people about the music that shaped their lives. Funny, raw, and full of great energy. You can find it by clicking channel 4 (CH4) on our radio.

What is Sedna FM

Sedna FM was born from the idea and passion of two best friends, Gaia and Yasmin. What started as a playful experiment soon became a creative journey, blending music, stories, and the spirit of adventure. Sedna FM is a space for curious minds, for those who love to travel with their imagination, and for anyone who believes that radio can still surprise and connect us.

Broadcasting from a different planet (at least in spirit!), Sedna FM brings together diverse sounds, cultures, and stories. Our mission is to inspire, entertain, and create a community that feels a little bit out of this world. Whether you're tuning in for the music, the stories, or just the vibes, you're always welcome on board Sedna FM.

Who We Are

Yasmin
Yasmin is a sport addict, travel maniac, and culture enthusiast. Half Moroccan, half Italian, she brings a unique blend of energy and curiosity to Sedna FM. Always on the move, Yasmin loves discovering new places, meeting new people, and sharing stories from around the globe. Her passion for music and radio is matched only by her love for adventure and her endless appetite for learning something new every day.
Gaia
Gaia is the creative heart and the sweet soul of Sedna FM. An untireable traveller, she is in love with the sea—both above and under it. Gaia's imagination knows no bounds, and her passion for storytelling brings warmth and depth to every episode. With a keen eye for beauty and a love for connecting with people, Gaia makes every broadcast a little more magical.
